Our Story

The journey of Spice Symphony

From Tradition to Innovation

Founded in 2010 by Chef Vikram Singh, Spice Symphony began as a small family restaurant in Mumbai with a big vision: to reinvent Indian cuisine for the modern palate while preserving its soul.

What started as an experiment with traditional recipes has evolved into a culinary movement that celebrates India's rich spice heritage through contemporary techniques and global influences.

"Food is not just about taste; it's about memories, emotions, and stories. At Spice Symphony, we're writing new chapters in India's culinary story." - Chef Vikram Singh, Founder
